What am I seeing Here? Below, you see the results of Metastudent, a method to predict Gene Ontology (GO) terms via homology to already annotated proteins ("Help" for a practical guide). GO terms are organized in two different ontologies, Molecular Function Ontology and Biological Process Ontology (details). Each predicted term is associated with a reliability (0-100), indicating the chance that the protein actually has this function. The graph additionally shows where the predicted terms lie with respect to their inferred parent terms. Predicted terms are shown in yellow boxes, inferred terms in white boxes.
If you want to highlight specific terms, de-select all others in the Tabular Result and press 'show'.
